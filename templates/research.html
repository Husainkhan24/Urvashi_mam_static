Research Areas

My research spans various aspects of the Gujarati language and literature, focusing on addressing key challenges in language preservation, linguistics, and cultural heritage. Here are my primary research areas

Research Work on Women Saint Writers of India

Our research focuses on the literary contributions of women saint writers in India, exploring their impact on cultural and religious discourse. We aim to analyze their works within historical, social, and spiritual contexts, highlighting their role in shaping literary traditions.

Research Work on Gender in Art

Our research delves into the representation of gender in various forms of art, examining how art reflects, challenges, and shapes societal perceptions of gender roles. We explore the intersection of visual culture and gender theory across different artistic movements and mediums.

Research Work on Ecocriticism & Art

We focus on the relationship between art and environmental themes, using ecocriticism to examine how artists address ecological issues. Our work explores the role of art in raising awareness about environmental challenges and its potential to inspire social change through visual expression.

Ph.D. Research in Modern Gujarati Fiction

Our research in modern Gujarati fiction aims to explore the evolution of narrative styles, themes, and social commentary in contemporary Gujarati literature. This includes an in-depth analysis of key authors and their contributions to the literary landscape, with a focus on modernity, identity, and cultural transformation.

UGC National Research Fellowship Award

The UGC National Research Fellowship Award is a prestigious recognition granted to scholars for their exceptional research potential in their respective fields. Our research under this fellowship focuses on advancing knowledge in the domain of Gujarati language and literature, with a special emphasis on the socio-cultural aspects and literary contributions of marginalized communities. The fellowship supports innovative projects aimed at addressing key issues and promoting deeper understanding in the field of language and literary studies.

My Publications

  • The Evolution of Gujarati Language: A Comprehensive Study
    Dr Urvashi manuprasad Pandya.
    Journal of Gujarati Linguistics, 2023, Vol. 22, pp. 78-102
    Abstract: This paper presents a comprehensive study of the evolution of the Gujarati language, exploring its historical development, linguistic features, and regional variations. We discuss key aspects of phonetics, syntax, semantics, and morphology, as well as the impact of socio-cultural factors on the language's evolution. The study also examines contemporary challenges in language preservation and revitalization efforts for Gujarati.
  • Ethical Considerations in Language Preservation: A Framework for Responsible Documentation
    Dr Urvashi manuprasad Pandya.
    Journal of Language Preservation and Ethics, 2023, Vol. 12, pp. 45-58
    Abstract: This paper proposes a framework for the responsible documentation and preservation of endangered languages. We discuss the ethical considerations in language preservation, including cultural sensitivity, community involvement, and the impact of technological tools. We also present case studies highlighting the application of our framework in the preservation of lesser-known languages, with a particular focus on Gujarati and its dialects.
  • Language Revitalization through Digital Tools: Progress and Challenges
    Dr Urvashi manuprasad Pandya.
    Journal of Language Technology and Preservation, 2023, Vol. 14, pp. 34-56
    Abstract: This paper reviews the progress and challenges in using digital tools for language revitalization. We discuss the latest technological advancements, including speech recognition, language corpora, and online platforms. We also highlight the ethical, social, and cultural considerations in the digital preservation and revitalization of endangered languages, with a particular focus on Gujarati and other regional languages.
